Piggsy is a simple app for tracking your savings goals.

You can create multiple piggy banks for anything you’re saving toward: a trip, a gadget, or just a rainy-day fund. Each one can have its own currency, optional deadline, and a short description so you remember what it’s for. Add or subtract money as you go and keep a history of your transactions, including small notes if you want to track details.

The interface is clean and easy to use, with support for dark mode and dynamic colors. Finished or paused goals can be archived so they don’t get in the way. You can also set up auto backups to never lose your data.
